About us

Tex Tech International is working as a Buying & Trading house in the field of RMG, Home Textile, Leather Products, Handicraft Items & other Consumers’ Products in Bangladesh. Our major concern is to play our role as a buying house of our customers. One of our primary responsibilities as a buying house is to assure that the product purchased and released for shipment conforms to be customer’s requirements.

Our goal is to provide our clients with high-quality products that meet their quality expectations. Tex Tech International employs Quality Assurance staff to assist in the selection of manufacturers who manufacture products according to Standards set by our policy, principles, and guidelines.

Tex Tech International is a leading Buyer’s agent / representative and garments exporter in Bangladesh. We have our own sourcing office in China, Italy, Netherlands, Canada & Australia.

We have 100+ associated factories from where we can assure quality and final products. All the associated factories are fully compliance having certificates of ACCORD, ALLIANCE, BSCI, SEDEX, WRAP, OEKO-TEX, GOTS, OCS, GRS, RCS etc. We have a strong CSR team who follow up on all the different types of compliance requirement which are set by the buyer.

We have a very strong Merchandising Team who keep contact and support the clients on the daily basis in order to keep a healthy relationship. Our QC teams is ready to ensure product quality as per customer demands. Our QA team inspect the goods on a third-party base and on AQL of customers.

We have a strong quality control system where under each Merchandiser there is a full time QC team and the QC’s do the in-line inspections in each section of production. In a particular time of the day, our QA Supervisor checks the 30% products those have been passed by our QC’s in each section. All the QC’s always check 100% goods. All the QC’s submit their daily reports to QA Manager with highlighting the major points. QA Manager submits their reports to the relevant Merchandisers in addition to CEO. Relevant Merchandiser ensures the correct QA packing to the QA Supervisor. After maintaining all the necessary procedures, the QA Manager carries out his own final inspection before the shipment.
