What type of client can approach Nafisa BD?

Retailer, Importer and Wholesaler.

What is the shipment lead time after the order placement?

60-110 days, depends on product and material sourcing, sampling, testing, approval, and inspection process.

What is the payment term?

L/C at sight and TT payment. Other options can be negotiable.

What the minimum order qty (moq) per colour/style?

Product wise it will vary. In general, per colour 2000-3000 pcs for Tee, Polo, Hoody, Joggers, Sweat, Pullover and Cardigan. But for woven Tops/Shirts, Long Pants, Short, Cargo, Jeans, Jackets, Swim shorts at least 3000-4000 pcs colour.
